We calculate total intermolecular interaction energy with this program.
In this program, we first make molecular coordinates files of molecular groups shown in the Suppelementaly Infromation.
Then we extract each molecukar pairs to be caluclated and calculate intermolecular interaction energy.

Molecular group are determined by their location, especially the sum of the abstract values of the coeficients of lattice vectors.
So we named this program from L1-norm concept.
